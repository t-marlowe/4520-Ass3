About This Website

Intent:

Welcome to McDavid's journey for 50 Goals, a website dedicated to tracking the progress of Connor McDavid, the Edmonton Oilers superstar and the best player in the NHL, as he tries to reach the elusive milestone of scoring 50 goals in a single season. Here you will find news, stats, highlights and analysis of McDavid's performance and his chances of achieving this feat. You will also learn more about his history and significance of scoring 50 goals in the NHL. Whether you are a fan of McDavid, the Oilers or hockey in general, this website is for you. Join us as we follow McDavid's journey to 50 goals!

Process:

My passion for hockey and admiration for Connor McDavid inspired the process for creating this website. I wanted the users to experience McDavid's journey from his upbringing right up to getting his 50th goal. To do this, I did extensive research on McDavid's life story, his career milestones, his achievements and his challenges. I used Figma for designing the website and some of the custom made SVGs that illustrate McDavid's progress and statistics. I also used Font Awesome for the icons that add some flair and functionality to the website. Additionally, I used JavaScript for the modal component that displays more details about this website. My goal was to create a visually appealing and informative website that showcases McDavid's quest for 50 goals in an engaging way.

Connor McDavid's Journey to 50 Goals

Who is Connor McDavid?

Connor McDavid is a Canadian professional ice hockey player who is the captain of the Edmonton Oilers in the National Hockey League.

He was born on January 13, 1997 in Richmond Hill, Ontario and started playing ice hockey against older children at a young age of 4.

He was selected first overall by the Oilers in the 2015 NHL Entry Draft and has since become one of the best players in the league. He has won several awards and honors, including the Hart Memorial Trophy, the Art Ross Trophy, and the Ted Lindsay Award. He is also known for his speed, skill, and vision on the ice.

The Significance of 50 Goals

Scoring 50 goals in the NHL is so significant because it is one of the most celebrated individual achievements in the league.

It is a rare feat that only 91 players have accomplished since the NHL began play in 1917, and fewer than half of those have done it more than once.

It is a mark of excellence, consistency, and skill that showcases a player's offensive prowess and dominance.

What's Next for Connor McDavid?

No matter the personal achievements, the ultimate goal for NHL players is the Stanley Cup. This will be the greatest challenge for McDavid, as winning the Cup is one of the hardest championships to win.

McDavid has averaged 0.79 goals per game this season. That's an impressive rate that puts him on pace to score more than 60 goals in a full 82-game season. He has scored 55 goals in 67 games so far and is projected to have 66 goals by the end of the season.

Many people agree that McDavid is the best player in the NHL right now, and has drawn comparisons to legends such as Sidney Crosby and Wayne Gretzky. He has also broken several records and achieved remarkable feats and has earned his reputation as one of the best hockey players of all time.
